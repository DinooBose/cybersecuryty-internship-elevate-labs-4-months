* ## CIA Triad with Real-World Examples

  The CIA triad forms the foundation of information security:

* ### Confidentiality - Ensuring information is accessible only to authorized individuals

    #### Banking Example:  Your account balance and transactions are visible only to you and the bank, protected by authentication and encryption Social Media Example: Private messages between users are encrypted and not visible to others

* ### Integrity - Maintaining accuracy and completeness of data

    #### Banking Example:  Ensuring your ₹10,000 transfer doesn't become ₹100,000 through data corruption Social Media Example: Preventing hackers from altering your posts or profile information

* ### Availability - Ensuring systems and data are accessible when needed

    #### Banking Example: ATM and online banking services should be available 24/7 Social Media Example: Platforms should be accessible without downtime from DDoS attacks
